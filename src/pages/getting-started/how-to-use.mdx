<PageDescription>

This site caters to three distinct users: product managers, designers, and developers. Each will have different paths for their journeys through the site. 

</PageDescription>

**Product managers** will gain insights into how adoption might work for their product. **Designers** will see more strategic design patterns across the whole of the canvas UI experience, while **developers** will review robust documentation to test and code their own experiences. 



<Accordion>
  <AccordionItem title="Product managers">
  
  Product managers can gain the knowledge they need from Elyra canvas by digging into the following:
    <br /><br />
   1. Read the introduction describing Elyra found <a href="./what-is-elyra-canvas">here</a>. 
 <br />
   2. Explore the design patterns found <a href="what-is-elyra-canvas">here</a>.
 <br />
   3. Interact with the test harness found at <a href="http://ibm.biz/elyra-canvas">http://ibm.biz/elyra-canvas</a>.
   <br />
   4. Use this site to gauge adoption with your team and introduce the patterns and technology.
   <br />  <br />

   
   </AccordionItem>
